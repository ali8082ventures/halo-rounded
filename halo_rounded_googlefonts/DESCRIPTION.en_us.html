Halo Rounded is a friendly rounded sans-serif designed for display use — headlines, apps, packaging, and playful brand identities. Its soft terminals and open, geometric forms give text a warm, approachable voice while staying clean and legible at large sizes.

Halo Rounded is a variable font with a Weight axis ranging from Light (300) to Bold (700), and supports over 200 Latin languages.

Halo Rounded was designed by Catherine Bellot and is published by Glyphline, an independent display-type foundry. To contribute, see github.com/ali8082ventures/halo-rounded.
